About Krishimitra

Krishimitra is an innovative agricultural platform designed specifically for Indian farmers. We combine artificial intelligence with local agricultural expertise to provide personalized farming solutions.

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology that was previously accessible only to large agricultural corporations. With Krishimitra, every farmer can make data-driven decisions.
